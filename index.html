Southeast Data Librarian Symposium 2025



The Southeast Data Librarian Symposium (SEDLS) is returning online in 2025! The program will consist of workshops, short presentations, and networking opportunities October 8-10, 2025.

The Call for Proposals is coming soon!

SEDLS is open to all who wish to attend, including students, data managers, and data scientists. Learn more about SEDLS on the About Us page.

This website will be updated with the latest information as planning continues. You can reach out to se.datalibrarian@gmail.com or the planning committee chair with any questions.
